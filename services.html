Services That We Provide

1. Group Discussion:

In this a topic will be given to the participants 2-3 days before the discussion. They have to prepare and express their views regarding the same. It will be done in both online as well as offline mode.

{It will be helpful in improving research and communication skills.}

2. Extempore:

In this the topic will be given 10-20 mins before the discussion and participants have to formulate and structure their views instantly.

{It is necessary for the development of a proper perspective and for instant decision making.}

3. Debates:

Similar to GD, Debates will also be of two types. In the first type topic will be provided 2-3 days earlier and in the second one topics will be provided instantly at the time of debate. Both of them have their benefits. It will help to improve communication skills, presence of mind, and diplomatic way of expressing viewpoints.

4. Mock interview

Participants will have to choose a profession of their choice. For that we will provide the 3-5 interviewers and a real interview-like atmosphere either online or offline so that they will gradually become used to the interview rounds

5. Speeches

In this segment participants will be given a topic and they have to prepare and deliver a speech on that topic. Here as well either the topic will be provided either 2-3 days earlier or instantly before the speech.

6. Presentation:

In this segment participants have to prepare a PowerPoint presentation and present it in front of the judges.

7. Seminars/ webinars:

Here we will be providing a seminar on the topic related to following domains and also regarding the above segments.
